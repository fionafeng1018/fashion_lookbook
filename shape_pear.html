Pear Shape

What is pear shaped body?

  • If you have narrow- shoulder
  • Well-defined waist
  • full-hips
  • Rounded bottom
  • Lower body wider than your upper body

How to dress?

Loose-fitting pants will be much more better. And for the upper body, they should wear something that is”heavy” then the lower body will look much more smaller. To be more simple, the dress for upper body should be more attractive and brighter than the lower body which need to be more simple and easy. Of course, black is the most popular color;

Pear-shaped body needs to avoid wearing tight-fitting T-shirts and jeans. This will reveal the shortcomings of pear-shaped body. As for me i never wear jeans and hardly ever wear pants, i like to wear dress more.

Instead, they need to wear loose pleated skirts or flowing skirts with high heels to cover their “shortcomings”.Of course, a tight dress on the top but loose on the bottom will be the best choice for them. Layering garments can balance a pear-shaped body by adding visual interest to the top half of the body. Embellish around the neck. Shirts and dresses with embellished necklines naturally draw the eye upward toward the slimmest part of a pear-shaped body.

Pear-shaped body needs to avoid wearing tight skirts, because it will makes them look wide. So you should:

  1. Never wear a tight jeans.
  2. A bright color pants.
  3. A skirt that is very tight .
  4. crop tops should be widely avoided for drawing attention to your midsection, which is too close to your hips to be flattering.
