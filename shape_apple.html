Apple Shpe

What is apple shaped body?

  • If you have rounded-shoulder
  • Have the fullness in the tommy area.
  • A good shaped legs
  • Feel uncomfortable when tops are tucked in

How to dress?

Apple shaped usually do not have obvious waistline, so they are more suitable for skirts with belts or skirts that highlight their waists. And they should choose something loose for the upper body, so they can show off their lower body. Keep the clothing line straight to slightly fitted, but fabric soft so you avoid unnecessary bulk around the bosom, waist and tummy Keep details above the bust line and below the hip line - for everything in between keep it plain and simple.The shoulders need attention so you should balance them from the front and side views.

Because the shortcomings of the apple shaped body are the waist,so it is necessary to avoid wearing tight-fitting skirts, especially skirts that do not show a waistline. And they should also avoid:

  1. Excessive fabric in the mid section
  2. Gathered or tiered skirts
  3. Clutch bags or shoulder bags
  4. Petite shoes, kitten heels, boots with buckles, Uggs boots
  5. Gathered or tiered skirts
  6. Clutch bags or shoulder bags
  7. Petite shoes, kitten heels, boots with buckles, Uggs boots

However, wearing v-neck top can soften these shortcomings. For the bottom, a pants with a belt which will emphasize the waistline will be the best choice for the apple-shaped body. And you should:

  1. Buy t-shirts that are ruched in the middle. This will confuse the eye and you can’t tell that there’s a big tummy underneath.
  2. Get Tops with a V-neck to break up the size of your chest and create a vertical line.
  3. Look for tops with a thicker texture that don’t cling so much. Woven tops can be great.
  4. The best height for tops is just belong your hipbone (when worn with pants).
  5. Patterns tend to look very good on a apple shape body as they flatter and camouflage.
  6. Tops should cover the whole belly area and end just below the hip bone.

Apple shaped body need to avoid wearing tight-fitting tops because they have rounded shoulder and full bust. And they should also avoid:

  1. Sleeves which finish next to your bust
  2. Any details, fuss and volume near bust, tummy or hip area e.g. no lapels, double breasted coats or jackets, high-waisted trousers or waistbands
  3. Skirt above knee length
  4. Clothing that finishes at your fullest points e.g. jackets, cardigans
  5. Excessive fabric in the mid section
